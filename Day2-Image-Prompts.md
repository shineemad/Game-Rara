# Prompt ChatGPT — Ilustrasi Dialog HARI 2 (Style Referensi Gambar 1)

> Tujuan: menghasilkan ilustrasi untuk **seluruh fase dialog Hari 2** dengan gaya seni
> yang **konsisten** dengan gambar referensi (anime hand-painted ala Studio Ghibli,
> latar Indonesia, pencahayaan hangat).
> Cara pakai: tempel **BLOK GAYA** + **REFERENSI KARAKTER** di awal, lalu tambahkan
> **prompt per adegan** di bawahnya. Untuk konsistensi maksimal, lampirkan gambar
> referensi (Gambar 1) ke ChatGPT/DALL·E setiap kali generate.

---

## 🎨 BLOK GAYA (tempel di SETIAP prompt)

```
STYLE: Studio Ghibli-inspired hand-painted 2D anime illustration. Soft watercolor
and gouache textures, warm natural lighting, gentle film-grain, painterly cel
shading with delicate gradients. Cozy, nostalgic Indonesian atmosphere. Detailed
backgrounds, soft rim light, warm earthy color palette (cream, terracotta, sage
green, dusty purple, maroon). Wholesome, child-friendly tone. No text, no UI,
no watermark. Cinematic 16:9 wide composition, eye-level.
```

---

## 👧 REFERENSI KARAKTER (jaga agar selalu sama)

```
RARA (protagonist): Indonesian girl about 10–12 years old. Black chin-length
straight bob hair with blunt fringe. Wears a purple ribbed knit turtleneck
sweater, a long pleated maroon/dark-red skirt, white socks, black mary-jane
shoes, and a brown leather school backpack. Gentle round eyes, calm and brave
expression. Petite build.

IBU RARA (mother, only home scene): wears a dusty-lavender floral hijab and a
matching pastel floral long dress, warm caring expression.

PRIA HALTE / PRIA ANGKOT (antagonist, stranger man): adult man, ordinary clothes,
wears a cap. Drawn NON-graphic and NON-scary — subtle "uneasy / too-friendly"
body language only. Keep it age-appropriate; never frightening or violent imagery.

PAK SUPIR (angkot driver): friendly middle-aged Indonesian man, simple shirt,
helpful expression.
```

## 🚌 PROMPT PER ADEGAN (urut sesuai fase dialog Hari 2)

### 1. Intro / Pamit di Rumah (opening)

```
Morning in a cozy Indonesian village home interior. Rara stands by the open
wooden door while her mother gently adjusts her backpack straps, smiling. Warm
sunlight streams in, a family photo on the wall, potted plants, tiled floor,
lush green garden visible outside. Tender farewell moment before school.
```

_(Adegan ini paling dekat dengan Gambar 1 — pakai sebagai patokan utama gaya.)_

### 2. Narasi — Jalan Menuju Halte

```
Rara walks alone down a quiet morning village street, backpack on, looking a
little cautious and alert. Soft golden sunlight, long shadows, empty road lined
with small houses and trees, a few distant figures. Slightly tense but calm mood.
```

### 3. Halte — Pria Asing Memperhatikan

```
A modest Indonesian roadside bus stop (halte) in the morning, a few commuters
waiting. Rara stands near the edge checking the time. In the background, a man
wearing a cap watches her from a distance with a too-friendly, slightly uneasy
posture. Warm daylight, realistic street details, non-threatening framing.
```

### 4. Angkot — Memilih Tempat Duduk

```
Interior of an Indonesian public minibus (angkot) seen from inside. Wooden/metal
bench seats along both sides, a few passengers including kind mothers with
groceries near the front by the friendly driver, and an empty dim corner at the
back. Rara stands at the entrance deciding where to sit, daylight through windows.
```

### 5. Sentuh Bahu — Insiden (versi AMAN, dekat supir)

```
Inside the angkot, Rara sits just behind the friendly driver near the door. A
strange man in a cap has moved to sit beside her, leaning a bit too close. Rara
looks uncomfortable but alert, glancing toward the driver for help. Keep it
age-appropriate, NON-graphic — only tense body language, warm daylight.
```

### 6. Sentuh Bahu — Aksi Berani (TIDAK / teriak)

```
Inside the angkot, Rara stands up firmly and raises a hand in a clear "STOP /
NO" gesture toward the man in the cap, brave determined expression. Other
passengers turn to look. Empowering, protective mood. Non-violent, child-safe.
```

### 7. ChatSim — Pesan WhatsApp dari Orang Asing

```
Cozy bedroom at evening, Rara sits holding a smartphone, looking worried at
incoming chat messages from an unknown contact. Soft lamp light, warm interior,
the phone screen glows on her face. Focus on her concerned expression. (No
readable text on the screen.)
```

### 8. Lapor — Minta Tolong Pak Supir (CERITA)

```
Inside the angkot, Rara bravely calls out to the friendly driver (Pak Supir) for
help. The driver turns and slows the minibus, concerned and supportive; other
passengers look over. The man in the cap shrinks back, caught. Relief and safety
mood, warm daylight. Non-violent, wholesome.
```

### 9. Tiba di Sekolah dengan Selamat (penutup)

```
Rara steps off the angkot safely in front of an Indonesian school gate in the
morning, relieved and smiling, backpack on. Other students arrive, friendly
guard nearby. Bright hopeful sunlight, lush trees, safe and cheerful atmosphere.
```

### 10. Kartu Edukasi — 3 Kata Sakti (opsional, poster style)

```
Warm wholesome educational poster illustration: Rara standing confidently and
smiling, gentle protective aura. Friendly, reassuring composition for children
about staying safe. Soft Ghibli-style painting, warm palette. (Leave clean empty
space for adding text later — TIDAK, PERGI, CERITA.)
```

## ✅ TIPS KONSISTENSI

- **Selalu lampirkan Gambar 1** sebagai image reference + tulis `match the art style,
character design, and color palette of the attached reference image`.
- Generate Rara **satu sheet karakter dulu** (depan/samping), lalu pakai itu sebagai
  referensi untuk semua adegan agar wajah & kostum konsisten.
- Pakai **seed / "same character" instruction** yang sama bila tool mendukung.
- Aspect ratio **16:9** agar pas untuk background scene game.
- Hindari kata yang memicu filter; jaga semua adegan **non-graphic, child-safe**.
